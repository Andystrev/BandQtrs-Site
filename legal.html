Let's Make Music

Bandqtrs is the ultimate collaboration platform for musicians. Whether you're in a band or working on solo projects, our app helps you connect, share, and create music together.

Features:

  • Send invites to musicians and form bands
  • Create songs and share ideas
  • Upload and collaborate on song files
  • Plan rehearsals, gigs, and music events
