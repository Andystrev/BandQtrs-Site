Welcome to BandQtrs! These Terms & Conditions govern your use of the BandQtrs mobile application ("App") and website ("Site"), available at bandqtrs.com. By using our services, you agree to comply with these terms.

1. Services Provided

BandQtrs is a mobile app designed for musicians to collaborate, create songs, upload song files, and manage band events such as practices and gigs. We offer free access with optional paid subscriptions: Headliner and Headliner Unlimited.

2. Subscriptions & Payments

  • Support Act – Free
    • Create unlimited bands
    • Invite your band members
    • Create events (practice, gigs, recording)
    • Access to the services lists and exclusive offers
    • Create songs & Setlists
  • Headliner – £2.99/$3.99/€3.99 per month
    • Includes all features of Support Act
    • Add your availability for events
    • Add unavailability for time periods
    • Upload song files to songs (5 x songs, 3 versions)
  • Headliner Unlimited – £4.99/$6.99/€5.99 per month
    • Includes all features of Headliner
    • Unlimited song file uploads and versions
    • Access to AI features (coming soon)

Subscriptions are billed monthly through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Your subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the billing period. You can manage your subscription in your device’s account settings.

3. Refund Policy

Subscription payments are non-refundable. However, if you experience technical issues, please contact support@bandqtrs.com.
